## Presentation MOV Export

The ProRes MOV presentation export uses `prores-wasm-encoder` to encode
transparent ProRes 4444 files locally in the browser.

`prores-wasm-encoder` is licensed under the MIT License and is installed from
npm.

## USDZ Backface Visibility

The USDZ exporter uses `polygon-clipping` for polygon boolean operations when
computing which back faces are visible from the rear side.

`polygon-clipping` and `splaytree` are licensed under the MIT License.
`robust-predicates` is licensed under the Unlicense.
